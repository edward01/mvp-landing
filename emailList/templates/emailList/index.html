A Network of Like-Minded Individuals

Most of today's social networks primarily strengthen existing relationships. Those that claim to "Match" strangers, don't understand the user enough to do it properly and don't offer a complete user experience. Our idea is to allow anyone to interact with others through their most important mutual interests that drive their day-to-day lives, so that connections are meaningful. With Panda, the experience is completely centered around the user.
