The Vision

Connect every person to every other person through common interests.

People thus far have used social networks primarily to strengthen existing relationships. Our idea is to allow anyone to meet anyone else through their common interests so that they can connect, collaborate, motivate the people connected to them and just be way more productive together.

Videos?

Video is the most engaging meaning of communication; hence the name Vidwork - the Video Network. When users are logged in, they will be able to create, upload and share videos with people or groups of people that are all passionate about the same thing.

A good way to think about our platform is as a mixture of Facebook, YouTube and the Stack Exchange (a group of community powered Q & A sites).

View details »

Difference from Facebook?

Facebook has had outstanding success in allowing people to connect based on their existing relationships. In our opinion, there is still a whole world of opportunity when it comes to meeting people from around the world without existing relationships. We intend to do this by linking like-minded people together through their common interests.
